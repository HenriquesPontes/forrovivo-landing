## 6. RIGHT TO RESTRICT PROCESSING (GDPR Article 18)

### What It Means
You can ask us to temporarily limit how we use your data while we verify or correct it.

### When You Can Request Restriction

**Scenario 1: Accuracy Disputed**
- You claim data is inaccurate
- We're verifying accuracy
- Processing paused during verification

**Scenario 2: Unlawful Processing**
- You believe processing is unlawful
- You want restriction instead of deletion

**Scenario 3: Data No Longer Needed**
- We don't need data anymore
- But you need it for legal claim
- We retain but don't use

**How to Request:**
1. Email: **privacy@forrovivo.com**
2. Subject: "Processing Restriction Request"
3. Explain: Which data and why
4. We respond within 72 hours

**Effect:**
- ‚úÖ Data stored but not used
- ‚úÖ You can still access your account
- ‚úÖ Essential features may be limited

**Duration:**
- Until issue resolved
- Or until you withdraw request

## 7. RIGHT TO WITHDRAW CONSENT (GDPR Article 7)

### What It Means
If we process data based on your consent, you can withdraw that consent anytime.

### What Requires Your Consent
- Analytics and usage tracking
- Crash reporting
- Marketing communications
- Personalized content
- Beta features

### How to Withdraw Consent

**In-App:**
1. Go to: **Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Consent Management**
2. Toggle OFF any consent type
3. Changes are immediate

**Email:**
1. Send to: **privacy@forrovivo.com**
2. Subject: "Withdraw Consent"
3. Specify which consent (e.g., "analytics")

### Effect of Withdrawal
- ‚úÖ We stop that processing immediately
- ‚úÖ Existing data retained per our retention policy
- ‚úÖ You can re-consent anytime

**Important:**
- Withdrawal does NOT delete existing data (use Right to Erasure for that)
- It only stops future collection

### No Penalty
- ‚úÖ Withdrawing consent does NOT affect your service
- ‚úÖ All features remain available
- ‚úÖ No price increase
- ‚úÖ Same quality of service
